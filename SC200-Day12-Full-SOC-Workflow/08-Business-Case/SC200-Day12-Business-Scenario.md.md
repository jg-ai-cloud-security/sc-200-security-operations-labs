\# Business Scenario



BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd is a growing small business that relies on Microsoft 365, endpoint devices, user identities, and cloud services to support daily operations.



As the organization becomes more dependent on digital systems, it needs a structured Security Operations Center approach to monitor activity, detect threats, investigate incidents, and respond quickly to suspicious behavior.



Microsoft Sentinel and Microsoft Defender XDR are being used to support a centralized security operations workflow across the environment.



This Day 12 lab represents the complete SOC process by bringing together monitoring, detection, investigation, and response into one end-to-end operational model.
