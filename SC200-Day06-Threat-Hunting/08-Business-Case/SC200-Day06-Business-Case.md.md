\# SC-200 Day 06 — Business Case



\## Company

BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd



\## Company Overview

BrightSpark Electrical Services is a small electrical contractor company with around 20 employees.



\## Business Scenario

The company wants to improve its security monitoring by proactively searching for suspicious activity instead of relying only on automatic alerts or incidents.



\## Business Problem

Some suspicious behaviour may not immediately trigger a high-confidence alert. This means weak signals could remain unnoticed unless the SOC analyst performs manual threat hunting across the available logs.



\## Security Goal

Use Microsoft Sentinel hunting queries and KQL to proactively identify suspicious authentication and security activity, validate findings, and strengthen the SOC investigation process.
