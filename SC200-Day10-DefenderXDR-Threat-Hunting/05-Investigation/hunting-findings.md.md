\# Hunting Findings



\## Objective

Review Microsoft Defender XDR Advanced Hunting and understand how SOC analysts use KQL to investigate suspicious activity.



\## What I Reviewed

\- Microsoft Defender portal

\- Advanced Hunting interface

\- Available hunting tables

\- Sample KQL queries

\- Query results and telemetry visibility



\## What I Observed

Today I reviewed how Microsoft Defender XDR supports proactive threat hunting through the Advanced Hunting experience. I explored the available tables and ran basic KQL queries to understand how telemetry can be queried across different security domains.



\## SOC Relevance

This capability is important because analysts can search for suspicious behavior even when a traditional alert has not yet escalated into a major incident.



\## Conclusion

Microsoft Defender XDR threat hunting improves SOC visibility and supports faster investigation through centralized security telemetry and KQL-based analysis.
