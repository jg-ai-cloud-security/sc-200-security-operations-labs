\# SC-200 Day 07 — Investigation Summary



\## Incident Overview



An incident was generated in Microsoft Sentinel based on suspicious authentication activity detected by an analytics rule.



The SOC investigation process was used to analyze alerts, entities, and related log data.



---



\## Investigation Steps



1\. Reviewed incident details

2\. Analyzed associated alerts

3\. Investigated related entities

4\. Examined the investigation graph

5\. Validated activity using KQL queries



---



\## Investigation Outcome



The investigation demonstrated the workflow used by SOC analysts to validate suspicious events.



The lab environment did not generate extensive authentication activity, therefore investigation results were limited.



However, the investigation workflow and detection validation were successfully demonstrated.
