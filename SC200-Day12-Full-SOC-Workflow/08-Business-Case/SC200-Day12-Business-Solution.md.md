\# Business Solution



To address the security operations gap, BrightSpark Electrical Services is implementing a Microsoft-based SOC workflow using Microsoft Sentinel and Microsoft Defender XDR.



The solution includes:

\- centralized monitoring through Microsoft Sentinel

\- alert and incident handling

\- investigation using entities, timelines, and logs

\- correlation with Microsoft Defender XDR

\- automation and response support through playbooks and workflows



This solution allows the business to move from isolated security activities to a connected end-to-end SOC model.



By combining detection, investigation, and response into one workflow, the organization can improve operational efficiency, strengthen threat visibility, and respond more effectively to incidents.



\## AI Enhancement



The organization can further enhance SOC operations using AI-assisted investigation aligned with Microsoft Security Copilot capabilities.



This supports:

\- faster incident summarization

\- suggested investigation paths

\- improved analyst efficiency



AI enhances the SOC workflow, but analyst validation remains essential.
