\# SC-200 Day 10 Business Case



\## Business Scenario

BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd is a small electrical contractor company with around 20 employees. The business uses Microsoft 365 services, user accounts, company devices, and email to support daily operations. As the company grows, it needs better visibility into suspicious activity across endpoints, identities, alerts, and email.



\## Business Problem

The company does not have enough proactive threat visibility. Security analysts need a way to investigate suspicious behaviour across multiple data sources instead of waiting only for high-severity alerts. Without centralized hunting capability, it is harder to detect early signs of compromise.



\## Business Risk

If suspicious activity is not identified early, the business could suffer account compromise, malware infection, email-based attacks, or unauthorized device activity. This could lead to operational disruption, data exposure, financial loss, and reduced trust in the company’s security posture.



\## Security Goal

Use Microsoft Defender XDR Advanced Hunting to proactively search security telemetry with KQL, investigate suspicious behaviour across multiple domains, and improve SOC visibility and threat detection capability.
