\# BrightSpark Electrical Services — Day 07 Business Case



\## Company Overview

BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd is a small electrical contractor business with around 20 employees. The company uses Microsoft 365, Entra ID, Microsoft Defender, and Microsoft Sentinel to support productivity and security operations.



\## Business Problem

BrightSpark needs a reliable way to investigate suspicious activity in its Microsoft environment. The business wants a structured process to review incidents, understand affected users or devices, validate evidence, and document response actions clearly.



\## Business Risk

If suspicious activity is not investigated quickly, BrightSpark could face account compromise, unauthorized access, endpoint infection, disruption to business operations, and possible exposure of company data. Without a defined incident investigation process, security issues may be missed or handled inconsistently.



\## Security Goal

Investigate and respond to suspicious security activity in Microsoft Sentinel by reviewing incidents, analyzing alerts and entities, validating evidence with KQL, and documenting clear response actions.



\## Day 07 Analyst Task

For Day 07, the goal was to practise the Sentinel incident investigation workflow. Because no live incidents were initially available, a manual test incident was created to simulate a real SOC investigation scenario.



\## Business Value

This process helps BrightSpark:

\- improve incident visibility

\- standardize analyst investigation steps

\- reduce delays in triage

\- improve documentation quality

\- strengthen operational security maturity
