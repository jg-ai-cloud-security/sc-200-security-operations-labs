\# SC200-Day09 Business Case



\## Client



BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd



\## Business Scenario



BrightSpark Electrical Services is a small electrical contractor company with around 20 employees. The company relies on user accounts, email, and cloud services for day-to-day business operations.



\## Business Problem



The company does not have a fast and consistent process for responding to security incidents. Manual response can be slow and may lead to delays or missed actions.



\## Business Risk



If incident response remains manual, threats may take longer to contain. This increases the risk of account compromise, operational disruption, and potential impact to business systems and data.



\## Security Goal



The security goal is to improve incident response by using Microsoft Sentinel playbooks and Azure Logic Apps to automate response actions and support a more structured SOC process.



\## Day 09 Relevance



This Day 09 lab is relevant because it shows how Sentinel playbooks can automate security workflows such as notifications, enrichment, ticketing, and response actions, helping the business respond more efficiently to threats.
