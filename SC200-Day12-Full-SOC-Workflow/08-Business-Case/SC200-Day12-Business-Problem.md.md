\# Business Problem



Although security tools may be available, the security process is not yet fully connected into a complete operational workflow.



Monitoring, detection, investigation, and response may exist as separate activities rather than one coordinated SOC process.



This makes it difficult for the organization to:

\- respond consistently to threats

\- investigate incidents efficiently

\- reduce analyst workload

\- operate with a mature security model



The business needs a unified security operations workflow that connects alerts, incidents, investigation, and response actions.
