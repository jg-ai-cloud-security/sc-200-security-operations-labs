\# SC-200 Day 08 Lab Steps — Sentinel Automation and Response



\## Lab Objective

Review Microsoft Sentinel automation capabilities and understand how automation rules and playbooks support incident response.



\## Lab Steps



\### Step 1

Open Microsoft Sentinel in the Azure portal and select the correct workspace.



\### Step 2

Go to the Automation section and review the available options.



\### Step 3

Review the Automation Rules page and check how rules can run on incident creation or incident updates.



\### Step 4

Review the Playbooks section and confirm that playbooks are based on Azure Logic Apps.



\### Step 5

Open an existing playbook or a template playbook and review the workflow design.



\### Step 6

Check the trigger configuration to understand what starts the playbook.



\### Step 7

Review the workflow actions such as notification, enrichment, task creation, or incident updates.



\### Step 8

Check run history or execution details if available.



\### Step 9

Document findings and explain how automation improves SOC operations.



\### Step 10

Upload notes, screenshots, and architecture evidence to GitHub.



\## Lab Outcome

This lab demonstrated how Microsoft Sentinel uses automation rules and playbooks to support automated incident response and improve SOC efficiency.
