\# SC200-Day11 Business Case



\## Organization

BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd



\## Scenario

BrightSpark Electrical Services is a small electrical contractor company with around 20 employees.



The business relies on Microsoft 365 email, user identities, and managed Windows devices for day-to-day operations. A suspicious security event has triggered alerts that may involve email activity, user sign-in behavior, and endpoint-related evidence.



The security analyst must review the incident in Microsoft Defender XDR to determine whether the activity is isolated or part of a broader attack chain.



\## Security Objective

Use the Microsoft Defender portal to review a unified incident, investigate alerts and affected entities, and document findings that would support real SOC triage and response.



\## Why It Matters

For a small business, a single compromised user account or device can quickly affect operations, finance, and customer trust. A unified investigation workflow helps analysts see the full picture faster and make better response decisions.
