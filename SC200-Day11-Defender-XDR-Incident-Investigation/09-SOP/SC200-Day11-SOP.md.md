\# SC200-Day11 SOP

\## Reviewing Microsoft Defender XDR Investigation Workflow



\### Purpose

To provide a repeatable process for reviewing the incident investigation workflow in Microsoft Defender XDR.



\### Scope

This SOP applies to Day 11 SC-200 lab activities in the Microsoft Defender portal.



\### Procedure

1\. Sign in to Microsoft Defender portal.

2\. Open Incidents under Investigation \& response.

3\. Review the incident queue.

4\. Open Alerts and review whether alerts are available.

5\. Check whether any entity investigation is possible.

6\. If no live data is present, document that the lab is a workflow validation exercise.

7\. Review Advanced Hunting if available.

8\. Record findings and save screenshots.

9\. Update GitHub, OneNote, and Excel tracker.



\### Output

\- Portal screenshots

\- Findings document

\- Architecture diagram

\- SOP

\- Summary

\- Tracker update
