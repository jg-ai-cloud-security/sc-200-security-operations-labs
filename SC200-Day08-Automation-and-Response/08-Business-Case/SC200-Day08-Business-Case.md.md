\# SC-200 Day 08 Business Case



\## Customer

BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd



\## Business Background

BrightSpark Electrical Services is a small electrical contractor company with around 20 employees. The business has limited internal IT and security resources and depends on efficient tools to monitor and respond to suspicious activity.



\## Security Challenge

Manual response to alerts and incidents can take time, increase analyst workload, and lead to inconsistent handling of security events.



\## Security Need

The business needs a more efficient and repeatable way to respond to incidents without relying only on manual processes.



\## Proposed Solution

Microsoft Sentinel automation rules and playbooks can automate incident response actions such as notifications, enrichment steps, task creation, and incident updates.



\## Business Benefit

\- Faster incident response

\- Reduced manual workload

\- Improved consistency

\- Better operational efficiency

\- Lower chance of missing important response steps



\## Conclusion

Using Microsoft Sentinel automation features helps BrightSpark Electrical Services improve security operations by standardizing and accelerating response activities.
