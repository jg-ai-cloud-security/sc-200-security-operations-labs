\# SOP — Full SOC Workflow with AI-Assisted Investigation



\## Purpose



Demonstrate a complete SOC workflow using Microsoft Sentinel and Microsoft Defender XDR, with simulated AI-assisted investigation support.



\## Steps



1\. Open Microsoft Sentinel

2\. Review workspace readiness and available incidents

3\. Analyze alerts and entities

4\. Use the investigation graph to understand relationships

5\. Run KQL queries to validate suspicious activity

6\. Review automation rules and playbooks

7\. Review Microsoft Defender XDR for related alerts or incidents

8\. Simulate AI-assisted investigation by summarizing findings and identifying next steps

9\. Validate all findings manually

10\. Document investigation outcome and architecture



\## Expected Outcome



The analyst can demonstrate a complete SOC workflow covering monitoring, investigation, validation, and response planning.



AI-assisted logic can improve investigation efficiency, but all findings must be validated by the analyst.
