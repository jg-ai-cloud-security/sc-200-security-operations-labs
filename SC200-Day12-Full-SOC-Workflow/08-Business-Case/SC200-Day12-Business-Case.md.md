\# SC-200 Day 12 Business Case



\## Business Scenario

BrightSpark Electrical Services Ltd needs a centralized SOC workflow to monitor, detect, investigate, and respond to cyber threats across its Microsoft environment.



\## Business Problem

Security activities are not fully connected into one end-to-end operational process, making investigations and response less consistent.



\## Business Risk

Without a complete SOC workflow, the business risks delayed response, fragmented investigations, and weaker overall security operations.



\## Security Goal

Create a repeatable SOC process using Microsoft Sentinel and Defender XDR to improve monitoring, investigation, and response.



\## Business Solution

Use Microsoft Sentinel and Microsoft Defender XDR together to deliver centralized monitoring, incident investigation, log analysis, and response workflow support.



\## Recommendations

Standardize the SOC workflow, improve investigation consistency, integrate Defender XDR, and continue tuning detections and response processes.
