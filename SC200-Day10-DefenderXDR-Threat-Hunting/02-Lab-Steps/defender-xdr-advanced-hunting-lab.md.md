\# Sentinel Playbook Response Lab



\## Objective



Review how Microsoft Sentinel playbooks use Azure Logic Apps to automate threat response actions in a SOC workflow.



\## Lab Steps



1\. Open Microsoft Sentinel.

2\. Navigate to the Automation or Playbooks section.

3\. Review available playbooks or templates.

4\. Open one playbook or Logic App workflow.

5\. Identify the trigger used by the playbook.

6\. Review the response actions configured in the workflow.

7\. Check whether the playbook can run manually, automatically, or both.

8\. Capture screenshots as evidence.

9\. Document the findings in the investigation and architecture notes.



\## Validation



The lab is complete when the trigger, workflow purpose, and response action have been reviewed and documented with supporting screenshots.
