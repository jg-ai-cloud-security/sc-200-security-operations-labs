\# Business Risk



Without a complete SOC workflow, the business faces several risks:



\- fragmented security operations

\- delayed response to active threats

\- inconsistent investigation outcomes

\- missed relationships between alerts, users, devices, and IP addresses

\- increased operational impact during incidents

\- reduced value from deployed Microsoft security tools



If these issues are not addressed, the organization may experience slower containment, higher business disruption, and a weaker overall security posture.
